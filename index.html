Booking system for auto detailers

Scheduling software for a car wash and car detailing businesses that allows you to increase sale, save time and stay organized all day long.

Why auto detailers need a booking system?

Save your time

52% of all appointments are made outside opening hours. Be available for online booking 24/7 – and your calendar will fill up on its own.

Increase customer bookings

Send automated email and SMS reminders to your customers to reduce the number of no-shows.

Manage your entire business

Mention reports, analytics, statistics, order history, customer database

What will I get?

Seamless website integration

Give your customers a seamless reservation experience on your website or Social Networks.

Customer Feedback

Use customer feedback to measure customer satisfaction and improve your existing services.

Client Database

View appointment history, personal details, notes, and purchase history so you’re prepared and ready for each appointment.

Reporting & Business Intelligence

View every customer’s payment history, track your revenue and quickly spot trends in your sales numbers.
